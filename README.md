On successful completion of this project, the following objectives need to be met:
•	Transition from raw, uncleaned multivariate data to a validated survival model through the execution of an end-to-end workflow.
•	Tell a data story in such a way that it educates the reader on the analytical workflow through the documentation of the logical steps taken to clean, audit, and prepare the data. Do not simply present results, explain the evolution of the data.
•	Provide actionable business recommendations.
